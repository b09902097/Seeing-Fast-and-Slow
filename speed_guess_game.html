Overview: Test Your Temporal Perception
This game challenges your ability to estimate video speed. You will be presented with short video clips playing at various slow-motion speeds. Your task is to use the preview slider to determine the precise speed factor that restores the clip to its true, real-time speed.

How to Complete Each Trial
1. Watch the video.
2. Adjust the preview slider to experiment with different speeds
2. Based on your observation, find the speed factor that makes the clip appear at normal speed.
3. Click "I think the video appears at normal speed now" to see how well your estimation is!
